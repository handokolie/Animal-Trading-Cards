Cat

The cat is a member of the cat family, the felids, which share a common ancestor such as lions, tigers and many others.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Lifespan: 2 - 16 years (in the wild)
  • Average Weight: 3.6 - 4.5 kg
  • Habitat: From deserts and scrub grassland, to dry and mixed forest

Cats are small, typically furry, carnivorous mammals. The average cat sleeps 16 - 18 hours per day. Most cats are lactose intolerant and should not be given cow's milk.
